Skybate Technologies Security Policy

1. Introduction

At Skybate Technologies, we are committed to safeguarding the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our data and systems, ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. This security policy outlines the framework through which we manage and protect our information assets.

2. Objective

The primary objective of this policy is to establish a robust security framework that ensures:

  • Protection of information and IT systems against unauthorized access, breaches, and vulnerabilities.
  • Compliance with relevant laws, industry standards, and regulatory requirements.
  • Continuous monitoring, risk assessment, and incident management to minimize security threats.

3. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and partners at Skybate Technologies who have access to our systems, networks, and information. It governs the use, transmission, and storage of data across the organization’s assets.

4. Security Framework

Confidentiality: Ensure that information is accessible only to those authorized to have access.

Integrity: Safeguard the accuracy and completeness of information and processing methods.

Availability: Ensure that authorized users have access to information and associated assets when required.

5. Access Control

  • User Authentication and Authorization: All systems and data access are restricted based on roles and responsibilities. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for access to sensitive data and critical systems.
  • Least Privilege Principle: Access is granted on a need-to-know basis, ensuring that users only have the minimum privileges necessary for their roles.

6. Data Protection

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption algorithms.
  • Data Classification: Information is classified based on its sensitivity (e.g., confidential, internal use, public), and controls are implemented accordingly to protect classified data.
  • Data Retention: Skybate Technologies follows strict data retention and destruction policies, ensuring that unnecessary data is disposed of securely.

7. Network Security

  • Firewalls and Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS): Firewalls and IDS are deployed to monitor and control incoming and outgoing network traffic.
  • Regular Audits: Network security audits are conducted regularly to identify and fix vulnerabilities.
  • Secure VPN: Remote access to Skybate’s network is only allowed through a secure Virtual Private Network (VPN).

8. Incident Management

Skybate has an established incident response plan to detect, respond to, and recover from security breaches. All employees are required to report security incidents or suspicious activities immediately to the security team.

9. Third-Party Management

  • Vendor Risk Management: Skybate Technologies requires all vendors and third parties to adhere to our security standards. We evaluate vendors' security practices through audits and risk assessments.
  • Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs): All third parties accessing Skybate’s data must sign NDAs to ensure the confidentiality of the information shared.

10. Training and Awareness

  • Employee Training: Regular security awareness training is provided to all employees to stay updated on best practices, emerging threats, and Skybate's security policies.
  • Phishing Simulations: Periodic phishing simulations are conducted to educate employees on identifying and avoiding phishing attempts.

11. Compliance and Legal Requirements

Skybate adheres to all applicable industry regulations and standards (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001) to ensure data protection and security. Regular internal and external audits are conducted to assess the effectiveness of security controls.

12. Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

Regular backups are taken and stored in secure, offsite locations. Business continuity and disaster recovery plans are in place to ensure critical operations can continue in the event of a security incident.

13. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually or when significant changes to the business, IT infrastructure, or regulatory environment occur. Any revisions to this policy will be communicated to all employees and stakeholders.

14. Conclusion

Skybate Technologies remains dedicated to maintaining a secure environment for our systems and data. By implementing this security policy, we aim to proactively defend against security threats, comply with industry regulations, and ensure the trust of our clients and partners.
