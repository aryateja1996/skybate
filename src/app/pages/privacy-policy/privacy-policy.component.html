1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal Information You Provide to Us: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when registering on our Services, interacting with us, or using our Services. This may include:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Contact details (phone numbers, billing addresses)
  • Location preferences
  • Payment information (handled securely by third-party payment processors)

Information Automatically Collected: When you interact with our Services, we may automatically collect technical data, such as:

  • IP addresses
  • Device and browser information
  • Operating system details
  • Usage data (e.g., pages viewed, actions taken)

Third-Party Sources: We may gather additional information from third-party sources, including public databases and social media platforms.

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

We use your data for several reasons, including:

  • To facilitate account creation and authentication.
  • To improve our Services and provide personalized content.
  • To communicate with you (e.g., customer support, updates).
  • To enhance security and detect fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
3. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We work with vendors and service providers (such as payment processors, analytics providers) to deliver our Services.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or sale, your data may be transferred as part of the business asset.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal processes or enforce our rights.
4. DO WE USE COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES?

Yes, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website and gather analytics. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. HOW DO WE HANDLE YOUR SOCIAL LOGINS?

If you choose to log in via social media, we will receive certain profile information based on your social media account settings (such as your name, email address, and profile picture). This data is only used as outlined in this policy.

6. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data. However, no security system is foolproof, and we cannot guarantee complete protection against cyber threats.

8. DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.
