Refund Policy

At Skybate Technologies, we strive to provide high-quality products/services and a seamless experience. If you are not entirely satisfied, please review our refund policy below.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Services: Refunds for services will only be issued if the service has not yet commenced, or in the case of dissatisfaction, after an evaluation of the issue.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Digital products/downloadable content
  • Customized or personalized products
